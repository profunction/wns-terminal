Stock Market News Terminal

WNS financial news terminal provides real-time stock market data, breaking financial news, cryptocurrency prices, and AI-powered market analysis. Track major stocks including Apple (AAPL), Tesla (TSLA), NVIDIA (NVDA), Microsoft (MSFT), Google (GOOGL), Amazon (AMZN), Meta (META), and 500+ more. Monitor market indices like S&P 500, NASDAQ Composite, Dow Jones Industrial Average, and Russell 2000. Follow cryptocurrency prices for Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), Cardano (ADA), and more. Get AI-generated trading signals, market sentiment analysis, support/resistance levels, and technical indicators including moving averages, RSI, and MACD.

Financial News Categories

Stock News: Earnings reports, analyst ratings, IPOs, stock splits, dividends, buybacks, and individual stock analysis.

Economy News: GDP growth, inflation data, Federal Reserve interest rate decisions, unemployment rates, consumer spending, and economic policy.

Markets News: S&P 500, Dow Jones, NASDAQ trends, oil prices, gold prices, bond yields, and overall market performance.

Cryptocurrency News: Bitcoin, Ethereum, blockchain technology, DeFi, NFTs, crypto regulations, and digital asset markets.

Technology News: AI breakthroughs, semiconductor chips, software, startups, and tech company earnings.

World News: International markets, geopolitical events, trade relations, and global economic developments.
